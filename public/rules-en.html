Daggerheart – Player Overview

Compact reference for the gaming table

🎲 Duality Roll

  • 2× d12 (Hope/Fear), higher die decides.
  • Pash (both equal) = critical success per rule text.
  • Interpret Hope/Fear in result narratively.

✨ Hope (Start 2, Max 6)

  • Generic: Help (1), Experience (1), Tag Team (3), Hope Feature (usually 3).
  • Timing: Generic Hope before roll; Features see text.

💨 Stress (Start 6 Slots)

  • Mark through moves, GM costs, opponents.
  • Full → Vulnerable; no space → instead of Stress 1 HP.
  • Reduce through Rest/Features (group agreements apply).

❤️ HP & 🛡️ Armor

  • HP: class-dependent; Armor: from equipment.
  • Damage over thresholds (Minor/Major/Severe → 1/2/3 HP).
  • 1 Armor slot → reduce Severe by 1 level (if allowed).

📌 Conditions

👁️ Hidden ⛓️ Restrained ⚡ Vulnerable
  • Hidden: Concealed, hard to detect. +2 to Stealth rolls, -2 to attacks against you.
  • Restrained: Held/bound. Cannot move, -2 to all rolls.
  • Vulnerable: Exposed/unprotected. +2 damage from all sources.
